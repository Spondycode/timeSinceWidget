# time_since_widget

This is a small widget-style application to count the time since a particular
date.

## Counting the days

In the gentle rhythm of life, days unfold like petals in a blooming garden. Each
dawn brings a fresh canvas, a promise woven with golden threads of time. We
count them not just as numbers, but as memories etched in the fabric of our
existence—milestones of laughter, tears, and growth. Every sunset marks the end
of a chapter, inviting us to cherish what has passed as we step into the unknown
of tomorrow. So, let us count the days, each one a jewel in the treasure chest
of our journey.

This application is to scratch a personal itch. I needed to know how many days
since a particular date, and I want it as a widget which will stay on top of my
Mac. I created it with Flutter and Dart, and the itch has been scratched.
